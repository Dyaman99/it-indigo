Find Us By the Buffalo River

Buffalo River Getaway offers a peaceful escape in the scenic upper Buffalo River region of Arkansas, where cozy cabin stays and outdoor adventure come together. From relaxing at Bear Ridge Cabin to planning a canoe trip on the Buffalo, guests can enjoy the natural beauty, quiet atmosphere, and memorable experiences that make this area so special.

  • Located in the upper Buffalo River wilderness
  • Cozy Bear Ridge Cabin with modern amenities
  • Canoe rentals and shuttling services from Ponca
  • Close to hiking, scenic river views, and wildlife watching
